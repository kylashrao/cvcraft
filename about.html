About

One clean resume, built the honest way.

CVCraft is a free resume builder for one job: help you produce a clean, professional, ATS-friendly resume without an account, a paywall, or a gallery of fifty near-identical templates.

Why one template, not fifty

Most resume sites lead with visual variety — colorful sidebars, icon-heavy skill bars, multi-column layouts. Many of those designs look great to a human and get scrambled by the applicant tracking software that actually screens resumes first at most companies. CVCraft uses a single, deliberately plain, single-column layout that's built to be read correctly by both software and people.

How the PDF export works

When you click "Download PDF," CVCraft draws your resume directly as text objects using a PDF library called jsPDF — it does not take a screenshot of the page. That means the text in your downloaded file is selectable, copyable, and extractable by ATS software, the same way a resume built in a word processor would be.

What we're honest about

This is one template with a small set of accent color options — not a design studio. If you need a heavily stylized, portfolio-style resume for a creative role, a different tool may suit you better. CVCraft is built for roles where clarity and ATS compatibility matter more than visual flair.

Your data

Everything you type is processed in your browser. Your draft is saved locally on your device using your browser's storage so you don't lose progress — it is never uploaded to a server. See our Privacy Policy for details.
