Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

CVCraft ("we," "us") operates cvcraft.app. This page explains what data is and isn't collected when you use the site, including the resume builder.

Your resume content is never uploaded

The resume builder runs entirely in your browser using JavaScript. The information you enter — your name, work history, education, and skills — is processed locally on your device to render the live preview and generate your PDF. It is never sent to, stored on, or logged by our servers.

Local storage / autosave

To prevent you from losing progress if you refresh the page, your draft is automatically saved using your browser's built-in local storage. This data stays on your device only, is not accessible to us, and is not synced across devices. Clearing your browser data, using a different browser, or using a different device will not carry your draft over. You can clear your saved draft at any time using the "Start over" button in the builder.

Cookies and advertising

This site may display advertising served by Google AdSense. Google and its partners may use cookies and similar technologies to serve ads based on your prior visits to this or other websites. You can opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings, or by visiting www.aboutads.info.

Analytics

We may use privacy-conscious analytics tools to understand aggregate site traffic, such as page views and general geographic region. This data is anonymized and is not linked to any information you enter into the resume builder.

Third-party links

This site may link to third-party services. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of external sites.

Children's privacy

CVCraft does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Continued use of the site after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
